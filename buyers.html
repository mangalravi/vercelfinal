Why B4Traders

B4Traders gives you access to high quality inventory with detailed manifest and published directly by brands and retailers. You decide how much you are willing to pay for the inventory on offer!

Step 1: Register Here

Choose the Brand or Retailer store you want to buy from. Read the terms of purchase and with few simple steps register for the store’s access.

Step 2: Get Approved

We will inform you as soon as your registration is approved. Our team will help you with the approval process

Step 3: Browse

View available inventory from top retailers and brands across conditions and at attractive prices from a variety of categories. You can use our filter for focused browsing

Step 4: Bid your offer

Place your bid. We will notify you if you have been out-bidded. Oh Wait! Like the inventory so much you want to buy right away? Easy – click on buy now price and we will take the inventory off the auction. Read the terms of purchase and with few simple steps register for the store’s access.

Step 5: Win

We will notify you if you have won. You can pay immediately and plan for pickup and Delivery.
